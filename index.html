About This Journey

This website documents my journey from complete beginner to (hopefully XD) competent ethical hacker and cybersecurity professional.


As a newbie, I'm starting with the fundamentals: networking, operating systems, programming basics, and security concepts. My goal is to build a strong foundation before diving into more advanced topics.


The name Protocol: ZERO represents starting from nothing (zero) and building up knowledge step by step, following established protocols and methodologies in cybersecurity.


This journey isn't just about technical skills—it's about developing the mindset of an ethical hacker: curious, persistent, detail-oriented, and always learning.
